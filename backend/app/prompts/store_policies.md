# Store Policies — ModestWear

This file holds the verbatim store policies the chatbot must cite
when answering policy questions. It is appended to the system prompt
on every request.

## Shipping

### US orders

- **Standard** (5–7 business days): $7.95 flat
- **Express** (2–3 business days): $19.95 flat
- **Free standard shipping** on orders over $100 (pre-tax, pre-discount)

### International

- We ship to most of North America, Europe, and parts of Asia.
- Rates are calculated at checkout based on destination and weight.
- **Customs duties and import taxes are the buyer's responsibility.**
  ModestWear does not pre-pay or refund these.

### Order cutoff

- Orders placed before 1pm US Eastern on a business day are processed
  the same day. After 1pm, they ship the next business day.
- We do not process or ship on US federal holidays.

### Lost or delayed packages

- Contact `support@modestwear.com` if your tracking has not updated
  in 7 business days. We will work with the carrier to locate the
  package or arrange a replacement.

## Returns

- **30-day return window** from the date of delivery.
- Items must be **unworn, unwashed, with original tags attached**.
- **Final-sale items** (clearance, intimates, accessories marked
  "final sale") cannot be returned.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–7
  business days of receiving the return at our warehouse.
- Buyer pays return shipping unless the item arrived damaged or
  incorrect. In that case, contact `support@modestwear.com` first.
- Start a return at `/returns` (you'll need your order number and
  email).

## Exchanges

- We do not currently offer direct exchanges. To swap a size, return
  the original item for a refund and place a new order.

## Sizing

- Sizes XS to 2XL across the catalog. Khimar is one-size.
- Full size chart at `/size-guide` (per product family).
- Between sizes? Size up for a more relaxed fit, down for a closer fit.

## Payments

- All prices are in US Dollars (USD).
- We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, Apple Pay,
  and Google Pay via Stripe.
- ModestWear **does not store card numbers** — Stripe handles the
  full PCI-DSS flow.
- For B2B / wholesale: payment is offline (Net-30 wire or check). See
  `wholesale_faq.v1.md`.

## Promotions

- Discount codes are entered at checkout. One code per order.
- Sale items are final-sale.
- We do not retroactively apply codes to past orders.

## Contact

- Email: `support@modestwear.com`
- Response time: within 24 hours on business days
- Hours: Mon–Fri, 9am–5pm US Eastern
